Sustainable Design

Why we work with rattan

“We just can't consume our way to a more sustainable world”

Sustainable design is about more than following the latest trend. Trends change but sustainable design is here to stay. Where previous generations of designers were less concerned by the environmental impact of their work, we take pride in designing for the future. A future where furniture helps nature thrive, instead of putting pressure on natural resources.

That’s why we employ cradle to cradle design in our work. Anyone can design a product… but taking into account the environmental impact of growing, harvesting, and transporting materials, and thinking about making even the end cycle of the product useful for nature, that’s what makes the difference.

Thanks to the initiatives by organizations like the WWF, local populations are also benefiting from growing and selling rattan. Rattan products are helping families in Laos and other third world countries overcome poverty. For example, since 2012, when they got involved in rattan weaving, collective income has more than tripled.

As a natural, sustainable, and resilient material, rattan is the logical choice for eco-friendly furniture. Compared to wood wicker in furniture, rattan is easier and cheaper to harvest, and safer for the environment. Not only that, but it also lends to beautiful and sophisticated designs, so it truly is a gift from nature.
