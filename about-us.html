About Us

Andreas Fredriksson

“Anyone can design. But to have the understanding of how to make it…that’s what makes the difference.”

Andreas Fredriksson is a Swedish, award-winning industrial designer, specialized in indoor and outdoor furniture, and product design. With decades of international experience, he harnesses his creative and practical skills to follow design projects from the initial sketch to the final product.

Jonas Hultqvist

“I’m passionate about using design to better the world, like guiding youth towards changing their mindset about design and using it to creatively solve social problems.”

Jonas Hultqvist, former facilitator for the Ikea Young Designer Awards and in-house designer for IKEA. He’s known for always trying to include an element of playfulness in his work. His love for living room furniture stems from the fact that is where he spends most of his time.

Willy Chong

“The trivial, the banal interests me immensely. It has its foundations in an understanding of how intuition works.”

Willy has been designing for IKEA since 2014 as a freelancer and as an in-house designer. He speaks 5 languages and likes to divide his time between Älmhult and Shanghai. He’s an alumnus of Beckmans College of Design and is known for reflecting his Swedish Chinese heritage in work.
