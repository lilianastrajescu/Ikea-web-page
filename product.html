Product

Designed for conscious consumers who take living sustainable and interior design seriously.

Gamlehult is built to last, lightweight, and its natural material gives warmth to your home. It’s simple, multipurpose design makes it ideal for smaller homes and minimalist styles, but it’s a great addition to any home. You can use it as a footrest, container, or extra seat, and it’s low enough to fit under most chairs. From design to production, every possible effort has been made to make Gamlehult as sustainable as possible. The footrest is easy to assemble: all you have to do is screw the legs together. Gamlehult is the most sustainable footrest you’ll find and the last one you’ll ever need.
